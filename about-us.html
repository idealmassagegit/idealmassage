About Us

Your body is working 24/7. Part of caring for your body is taking a moment to pause and allow the body time to restore and renew. Bodywork allows your body to take that pause. Since no two individuals are the same, no two sessions should be the same.  Each service is customized to your needs.

Our massage therapists are committed to excellence, and we take pride in delivering personalized and dedicated service. If you have been considering massage, now is the time to take care of yourself so you can feel like you are truly living and enjoying life!

What To Expect

A Licensed, Certified, and Registered Massage Therapist will begin the process with a discovery consultation in which we will discuss your massage preferences and areas you’d like us to place focus. Based on our conversation and your current physical condition, we will craft a treatment designed specifically for you and your needs. During future visits, our Massage Therapist will ask about any changes to ensure we are tailoring your service as needed. If you are new to Ideal Massage, we encourage you to check out our FAQ’s for more information regarding your visit.
