The focus of my massage practice is on therapeutic bodywork with a highly relaxing quality within a holistic, mind-body-spirit perspective. My healing work varies greatly, intuitively drawing upon many different healing traditions to meet the specific conditions that arise. My practice centers around listening to your body's messages and you, because you are the expert of your own body. Although I am a total massage geek, I still feel that caring, listening, intuition and tuning into energy are my best tools as a healer. Violet DeLuna

Violet takes a therapeutic and clinical approach to her massage therapy. It’s important to her that she works as a team with her clients to get their body feeling restored and working optimally. If that means full body therapeutic massage or more orthopedic massage that works just one area, Violet is here to help. A good massage will help the body return to a state of normalcy and increase functionality of the muscle and other tissue structures. It is her hope to facilitate a pain-free, balanced state of being for each client. She uses an integrated and compassionate approach that creates massage that is just for you.
