Staff

Jeanette

Massage Therapy has opened the doors for me to provide a holistic service to people who not only wish to relax, but also seek help to address issues of tension, tightness, and pain. I have provided massages to individuals from Chicago to New Jersey and I am excited to continue my path here in Texas. My favorite modalities and techniques involve trigger point therapy, hot stones, aromatherapy, and deep tissue. I believe in addressing the body’s stresses in a therapeutic setting with respect to your patience and tolerance with your personal healing journey. Our bodies are our vehicles that we must take care of, and my passion lies in helping you gain a better sense of wellbeing so that you may live a healthier life. Let me help you get there.

Cindy

I became intrigued with the healing arts upon discovering acroyoga (partner acrobatics), in which responsive connection and positive touch are essential. After learning Thai massage techniques as a practicing acroyogi, I pursued LMT certification. I studied at The Lauterstein Conway School of Massage and am continually expanding my knowledge base through reading and classes. I enjoy helping people reach a state of deep relaxation, as well as aiding mobility and healing. Educating my clients in self-care helps them extend the relief found in their sessions. I find fulfillment working with clients recovering from surgeries or injuries to help them regain their range of movement as well as with people going through intensely stressful times in their lives to help them reach a state of restorative relaxation.

Candice

Enthused with anatomy knowledge, combined with her personal experience Candice provides a strong, firm touch integrated with an intuitive energetic sense of YOUR body mechanics. Her experience and education offer a wide variety of modalities and techniques which include Deep Tissue, Sports Massage, Chair Massage, Pregnancy Massage, Therapeutic Touch, Reiki Master, and Hot Stone Therapy. Candice is very professional and has a genuine care for her clients. One client has already stated “Her attention to detail and ability to find my "trouble" areas without even telling her exactly where to concentrate is amazing.” Candice is bookable Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday of each week.

Chelsey

Structural Integration and Advanced Body Work are the center point of my therapy focus. It is based upon my desire to reach others on a theraputic, yet rehabilitative level. Ensuring that my clients have regained optimum range of motion while providing additional guidance in recovery stretching and technique, is one of the major benefits of being a client. My hope is that you will leave your session having a new found knowledge, and a greater respect for the healing nature of massage.
