Staff

Michael Neely

BILINGUAL BOOKING OPTION!

The focus of my massage practice is on therapeutic bodywork with a highly relaxing quality within a holistic, mind-body-spirit perspective. My healing work varies greatly, intuitively drawing upon many different healing traditions to meet the specific conditions that arise. My practice centers around listening to your body's messages and you, because you are the expert of your own body. Although I am a total massage geek, I still feel that caring, listening, intuition and tuning into energy are my best tools as a healer. I look forward to getting to know you and the miraculous ecosystem of your unique body.

Violet DeLuna

Violet currently lives in the Manor area with her husband, Ever, and their two rescue dogs Mike and Lola. During her downtime Violet enjoys reading, playing video games, and exploring the local craft beer scene. She’s a second-generation Massage Therapist. As the daughter of a massage therapist, Violet learned techniques at a young age that would fuel lasting relationships with her clients down the road. After spending 5 years in technical support, she realized that she might feel more fulfilled in the massage therapy field. Violet takes a therapeutic and healing approach to her massage therapy. It’s important to her that she works as a team with her clients to get their body feeling restored and working optimally. If that means relaxation massage or deep tissue work, Violet is here to help.

Chelsea Luce

Structural Integration and Advanced Body Work are the center point of my therapy focus. It is based upon my desire to reach others on a theraputic, yet rehabilitative level. Ensuring that my clients have regained optimum range of motion while providing additional guidance in recovery stretching and technique, is one of the major benefits of being a client. My hope is that you will leave your session having a new found knowledge, and a greater respect for the healing nature of massage.
